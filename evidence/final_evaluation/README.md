# Final evaluation evidence

`reproducible_policy_v1.json` is the compact, repository-safe record of the
2026-07-21 one-shot local verification. Full generated CSV/JSON/Markdown/SVG
artifacts remain under `~/.ros/robot_agent/` and are bound here by SHA-256.

The 42 cases are deterministic contract and policy replay. They do not claim
that a live MiMo request or ROS graph was exercised during this run; live
evidence is stored in the other `evidence/` directories.
